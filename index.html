Hi, I'm Linda!

I am a UI Engineer at We Also Walk Dogs, Inc. (ActionKit.com), where I work on user interfaces used by progressive campaigners and occasionally fix bugs in Python and Django.

I enjoy sharing knowledge and learning with others, and host a Women Who Code meetup every other week in the Bay Area. You might also often see me scheduling spontaneous learning hangouts on CodeBuddies.org, an open-sourced, free and friendly community of independent code learners that I started in 2014.

Outside of working on becoming a better craftsman of the Internet, I enjoy arranging and recording music that gets stuck in my head (and putting the sheet music in the videos), taking scenic photos, following news about the intersection of fandom + technology + civic engagement, and watching too many re-runs of Star Trek: The Next Generation.

I cannot decide whether I am a Ravenclaw or a Hufflepuff.
